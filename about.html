We help businesses grow using tech products

Space Development is an IT outsourcing company: we build custom mobile and web apps, refine existing solutions and help with user acquisition. We are needed, when businesses have ideas for tech products, but lack the skills to actually create them. Or when the current development team needs additional support.

With our help, an abstract idea turns into a real revenue channel, while existing projects become more stable, convenient and scalable.

Revenue and other business metrics improvement

How we work

We value reputation and relationships. Our customers' goals are more important to us than our own. That's the only way to earn long-term trust. We use budgets sparingly, without artificial projects stretching, and common, proven solutions instead of innovation for innovation's sake.

Our core values

1. Responsibility

We fully own our decisions and suggestions. Shifting responsibility to the client for technical solutions is what we avoid at all cost. If we propose an idea, we also take on the responsibility for its results.

2. MVP-first

We do not propose to create a complex, large-scale and expensive product without first testing the idea. Based on our experience, we've found that progressing iteratively is more effective. It not only helps in avoiding errors but also allows for reinvesting the profits from the product back into its development. This is the most effective approach.

3. Involvement

We don't go after loads of projects at once. For us, being fully involved in each client's work is key. But that can't happen if our developers are juggling 4–5+ teams each. We always make sure that each specialist involved in 1–2 projects max, clearly understands their roadmap, milestones and desirable results.

Our story:

The company's history began in 2020. By this time, the company's founders, Kirill Tupikau and Emil Agaeu, already had extensive experience in software development and marketing, including on projects with global brands. They decided to leverage their expertise to create their own tech business.

2020

Company establishment. First own mobile product MVP launch. Creation of the first custom product for a HoReCa client.

2021

Space Development entered the Health Founders accelerator program. Company's own product passed the 500,000+ downloads mark. The team had grown to 20+ specialists.

2022

Initiating partnerships with AI startups in the fields of dermatology and cosmetology. Launch of 3 additional own mobile products. Chatbots and digital promotional platform building for clients in e-com and retail spheres.

2023

Projects for clients in Games & Entertaiment and Business Tools domains. The company's own products had broken the 2,000,000 downloads mark. The team had grown to 30+ people.
