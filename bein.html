Client

BeIN - one of the world's largest sport channel networks, with an audience of 50+ million paying subscribers. It operates
34 channels, is available on 5 continents, broadcasts in six languages and has 30+ million social media followers worldwide.

Mission

The client was looking for the creation of an up-to-date mobile platform for its viewers. The main goals were to create a new revenue stream, increase customer loyalty and diversify the client's business overall.

Main requirements for the solution included the highest level of security and support of a wide range of content formats. Precise localisation, with high attention to cultural differences in the UX, was also part of the case.

Challenges

Development of a state-of-the-art security system that prevents broadcasts piracy. High attention for cultural differences is UX, especially for the Arabic region.

Solution

The solution consists of 2 mobile apps: BeIN Sports and BeIN Connect.

BeIN Sports is a sports news app. It provides users with news, statistics, matches, tournaments, broadcasts schedule and other features that all the fans need. The app includes wide personalization capabilities: from choosing of favorite teams to algorithmic newsfeed and push-notifications.

BeIN Connect - streaming platform with sport broadcasts, available on almost any device. It provides users with the opportunity to watch matches live on any device. Built-in advanced 3-layer security system prevents broadcasts from piracy and protects clients' revenue stream.
