DeliveryBot

Set of chatbots, CMS and CRM system for restaurant delivery service.

Impact on clients business

0

users

Chatbot brings revenue and new consumers for client in 20+ restaurants

Client

Restaurant chain in Eastern Europe with more than 20 restaurants of Asian and Mediterranean cuisine.

Mission

The project was launched during the most acute phase of the COVID-19 pandemic. The client's task was to organize a restaurant's delivery service from scratch during lockdowns.

The existing solutions available in the market did not fit into the client's economic model due to high commissions and other costs. This led to a need for an in-house IT platform to ensure smooth business operations.

Challenges

COVID-19 recession and deadlines.
Need for cost-effective solution.

Solution

The set of services for meal delivery, work processes optimization and CX improvement.

The user-end part of the solution was created in the form of a chatbot in messenger apps. It provides them with access to the menu, allows them to place an order and make an online payment. Using messengers to implement the solution reduced customer acquisition costs and accelerated the launch of the service, which was especially important during the pandemic. The bot also includes feedback and upsell functionality.

CRM and CMS systems have been created for restaurant administrators. They provide efficient order management, feedback processing and the ability for quick menu changes.

Chefs and kitchen staff received an order notification system via a separate chatbot, which reduced the need for personal interaction and helped them comply with social distancing rules.

Key Features

The ability to take orders and distribute it to cooks
To view the menu and order delivery
The ability to specify the delivery address and make online payment
To add promo items and meals
The ability to view and receive all customer orders online
To assign orders to couriers and track order statuses
