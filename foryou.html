Client

ForYou - an Estonian startup in Wellness & Beauty domains. The company has successfully completed Health Founders acceleration and has grown x5 in Users within two years. Now the startup is developing several applications, pursuing a plan to create a unified beauty and wellness platform.

Mission

The client was looking for an all-in-one solution for a beauty market, a «digital beautician in the smartphone».

The main goal was to create an app, which helps users with all of their home skincare needs - from skin moisturizing to kinesiotaping. The major requirement was to make core mechanics as interactive as possible. And keep the UI clean and friendly, even with a big amount of features inside the app.

Challenges

Creating clean, simple and friendly UI.
Making all the mechanics as interactive as possible.

Solution

An app helps users to apply skincare products the right way, make face-massages and do face-fitness safely and effectively. Additional minor features include progress tracking, water intake and anti-stress breathing techniques modules.

The Built- in content module provides an opportunity for micro-learning with articles and short stories - made in-house or by clients» partners.

Face-massage mechanics are made using AR. Additionally, all the user photos (cleaned from all the sensitive data) are analyzed using AI.
