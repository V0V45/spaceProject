My Bedroom Guide

Intriguing online game for couples, with engaging web-based UI, personalisation and 200+ playing scenarios.

Impact on clients business

Business expansion on 50+ countries

Subscription based model and creation of predictable revenue stream

Client

CreateMe LTD, an UK-based company in the entertainment domain. Since 2018, the company has been developing and selling its own adult board game for couples. The company is represented in Europe and the Middle East.

Mission

The client was looking for a dedicated team to digitize an existing project - an erotic card game for couples. The game offers users a variety of scenarios to make players sex life more intense and diverse.

The team's task was to produce a digital version of the product. Main clients requirements included a fast, web-based UI and effective user engagement elements to ensure a predictable subscription growth.

Challenges

Need for a clean and fast web-based UI
Achieving of high engagement and low churn rates

Solution

The created solution is a web-based Flutter app.

The product helps users to play out different sexual scenarios, using digitized gaming cards. Additionally to core mechanics, we added points, badges and levels system. It helps to make the game even more exciting and encourage users to play the game regulary. An authorization module helps to save the game progress. And social media sharing and calendar integration helps to plan all of the activities ahead.

Users can access the game from both mobile and desktop devices. Thanks to the use of Flutter, the product looks and functions equally well on all of them.
